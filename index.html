What is Cyberbullying?

Cyberbullying is the use of digital platforms to harass, threaten, or embarrass others. It can happen through social media, text messages, and other online interactions.

Explore this website to understand its impact and how you can prevent it.

Why It Matters

In a world where online presence is more prevalent than ever, understanding and addressing cyberbullying is crucial for creating a safer digital space.
