The Evolution of Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying has evolved alongside the internet, starting with anonymous chat rooms and now extending to every major social media platform.

  • 1990s: Early chat rooms and message boards.
  • 2000s: Social media platforms like MySpace and Facebook.
  • 2010s & beyond: Rise of Instagram, TikTok, and more.
